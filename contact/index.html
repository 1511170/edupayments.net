Frequently Asked Questions

The goal is to answer the most frequently asked questions relating to our payment service. If you don't find the information you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact support.

What payment methods does Edupayments accept?
Edupayments accepts bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and local payment methods depending on your country.
How secure is my payment information with Edupayments?
We employ advanced encryption, fraud detection, and comply with international security standards including AML and KYC regulations.
Can I get a refund for my payment?
Yes, refunds are processed according to our refund policy. Please contact our support team for specific cases.
How can I track my payment status?
You can track your payment status in real-time through your Edupayments dashboard or by contacting support.
What should I do if I encounter an issue with my payment?
Please contact our support team at support@edupayments.net and we will resolve your issue promptly.
Who registered Edupayments?
Edupayments is registered as an Independent Remittance Dealer (IND100584354-001) in Australia.
