The features that make our service unique

Automatic Earnings

Set up services and commission rules once; every student payment auto‑attributes the sale to your agency and pre‑calculates your share (tracked by link/code). Real‑time alerts and ledger entries in your dashboard.

Multi-user Access

Grant access to team members in different regions with custom permissions.

Automatic & Instant Commissions

Edupayments automatically deducts your commissions and transfers the remaining balance to institutions—no hassle, no manual work.

Do-it-yourself

Create payment links, define concepts/amounts/currencies, and manage funds without tickets or delays—operate on your own time.

Online Support PDF – Payment to the Institution

Generate an official PDF with student/agency/institution details, total amount, agency commission, amount transferred, timestamps, payment method, and transaction ID—useful for audits, visas, scholarships and internal control.

Global Payments & Refunds

Send and receive payments effortlessly in 100+ countries, without complications.
