Edupayments and Monoova are transforming global payments for the education industry

Australia has the second-highest share of international students globally. In 2024, there were over a million international student enrolments, a 15% increase year on year.

While studying overseas can be an exciting prospect for students, navigating tuition and living expenses across borders can be challenging and stressful due to currency fluctuations, transaction fees, security concerns and more.

Monoova explores the key challenges of cross-border payments for international students and examines how innovative payment solutions can help alleviate many of these challenges, while also helping to improve the overall payment experience.

Key Payment Challenges for International Students and their Agencies

Navigating the complexities of international payments can be a daunting task for students studying abroad, from making payments for tuition and rent to managing daily expenses. For businesses such as education agencies, cross-border transactions often come with administrative burdens, whether it's managing and processing multiple student payments or handling global payroll.

Founded in 2021, Edupayments is an Australia-based, global payments solutions provider for the education sector. Edupayments simplifies the foreign exchange process and facilitates cost effective and seamless transactions for educational agencies and their students.

Edupayments leverages Australia's payments infrastructure to enable students to transfer funds between Colombia to popular study destinations, including Australia, Canada, the UK, New Zealand, Ireland, Spain, France, Malta, Germany, and the United Arab Emirates.

The Edupayments platform provides a streamlined payment experience for education agencies to easily and securely manage student payments, facilitating 24/7 global funds transfer and competitive exchange rates for students.

How Edupayments tackles the top 6 challenges faced by international students

  1. High Fees & Exchange Rate Markups

Traditional banks can charge multiple fees (sometimes hidden!) when processing cross-border payments. The currency conversion fee which is typically calculated as fixed percentage of the total transfer amount can be a significant cost when transferring large amounts of money.

Solution:

Edupayments enables students and agencies to save money on international payments by eliminating transaction fees and offering competitive exchange rates. Through strategic investment in technology, Edupayments streamlines processes and reduces operational costs, passing these savings directly to its customers.

  1. Slow Processing Times

Bank transfers, particularly SWIFT payments, can take several days to process, causing delays in tuition payments, rent, or other essential transactions.

Solution:

Edupayments provides a smoother, quicker, and more secure payment experience as opposed to sending funds directly from Colombia. By leveraging Australia's advanced infrastructure for global payments, funds are collected locally in Colombia using PSE, their most popular payment gateway, and the payments are processed from Australia through Monoova APIs.

  1. Limited Payment Options

Many students struggle with limited payment methods when paying tuition or living expenses abroad. Some banks may not support international transactions, and credit cards often come with high foreign transaction fees.

Solution:

Edupayments offers multiple payment options, including local bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and alternative payment methods, giving students flexibility in how they pay.

  1. Security Concerns

International students are often targets for fraud and scams due to unfamiliarity with local banking systems. Additionally, sharing sensitive banking details across borders can pose security risks.

Solution:

Edupayments employs advanced encryption, fraud detection, and compliance with international security standards to ensure that all transactions are secure and protected.

  1. Lack of Transparency

Hidden fees, unclear exchange rates, and unexpected charges can make budgeting difficult for international students who need to manage their finances carefully.

Solution:

Edupayments provides full transparency on fees and exchange rates upfront, allowing students to know exactly how much they will pay and receive before confirming a transaction.

  1. Complex Documentation

International payments often require extensive documentation, including proof of enrollment, visa details, and compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, which can be overwhelming for students.

Solution:

Edupayments simplifies the documentation process by integrating with educational institutions and using automated compliance checks, reducing the burden on students while ensuring regulatory requirements are met.

Conclusion

By addressing these key challenges, Edupayments is transforming the way international students and educational agencies handle cross-border payments. With faster processing, lower fees, enhanced security, and greater transparency, Edupayments is making global education more accessible and affordable for students worldwide.
